Who were the signers?

famous signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Decloration of independance include-

John Hancock
Known for his large, bold signature and as the President of the Continental Congress.
Thomas Jefferson
The primary author of the Declaration and later the 3rd President of the United States.
Benjamin Franklin
A prominent statesman, inventor, and diplomat.
John Adams
A strong advocate for independence and later the 2nd President of the United States.
Samuel Adams
A key organizer of the Boston Tea Party and a political leader in Massachusetts.
